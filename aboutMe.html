About Me

Hello, I'm Kalen Weinheimer,
I'm a recent graduate of The University of Montana, with a B.A. in Media Arts, and a beginner game designer / developer, with a particular interest in level design, environmental storytelling, and world building.

I have experience developing game projects with C# (in Unity) and JavaScript, and while I may not have much professional or show-worthy work just yet, I'm constantly working to learn more about game design and get better at game development.
I am currently looking for an introductory job within the game industry as a game tester to learn more about game design and development, while also working on personal projects in my free time.


I don't have a ton of personal details to share, but, I grew up in a small town near the middle of Montana, and I have been playing video games since around middle school.

Over the years, I have found that what I enjoy most about most about video games is exploring expansive worlds, searching for secrets / hidden details, and the feeling of adventure they can give. Due to this, I care most about the unique interactability of the medium, and how this can create experiences not possible in any other form of media. I place importance on gameplay above most any other aspect of a game, and think in terms of how other aspects can be used to further enhance the gameplay.

However, I believe the best method of conveying what I value in a game is to list some of my favorite games and game genres.



Starting with favorite game genres, I tend to most enjoy RPGs, Immersive Sims, Sandbox Games, Puzzle Games, Rogue-Likes, and Retro-Style (/Boomer) Shooters.
